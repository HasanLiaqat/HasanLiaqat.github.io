About Me

Security is a
mindset, not a tool.

I'm a cybersecurity student driven by a genuine curiosity for how systems work — and how they can be compromised. Every exploit I study, every CTF challenge I solve, and every tool I build gets me closer to understanding the full picture of digital security.

My approach is hands-on: I'd rather spin up a vulnerable VM and practice privilege escalation than read about it. I believe ethical hackers make better defenders, and I'm working toward becoming one.

Currently sharpening my skills through TryHackMe, personal projects in C++ and Python, and reading everything I can about real-world attack chains and remediation strategies.
